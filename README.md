# Space Safari — Lineup

An unofficial companion app for **Space Safari Festival 2026** (4–6 September, Massembre, Belgium) — a fast, offline-first way to browse the full lineup, build your schedule, and not miss the sets you care about.

## Features

- **Full lineup** across all 4 stages, with search and filters by genre, country, and label
- **Favorites** — heart the acts you don't want to miss, and back up your list as a shareable code or QR
- **Reminders** — get notified before a set starts, or export it straight to your phone's calendar
- **Live weather** — current conditions plus an 8-day forecast for the festival site
- **Artist info** — bios, photos, genres, and links to Spotify, Bandcamp, YouTube, SoundCloud, Instagram, Facebook, and Discogs where available
- **Discovery mode** — browse artists you haven't checked out yet, or let the app surprise you with one according to the artists favorited
- **Compare with friends** — see which sets you and your crew all want to catch
- **Dark/light theme**, plus a full color-scheme picker if the default isn't your vibe
- **Works offline** — install it once and it keeps working all festival long, no signal needed

## Installing

You don't have to install this — it works perfectly in your browser via the link. But on iOS, if you don't install it, Safari may automatically clear your saved favorites after about 7 days of not opening the page (a privacy restriction from iOS itself, not something this app does). Installing to your Home Screen avoids this.

Add it to your phone's home screen so it opens full-screen and works offline:

- **iPhone** — you have to use **Safari** (iOS only lets you install to the Home Screen from Safari). Tap the **•••** button, then **Share**, then **Add to Home Screen**. If you don't see that option right away, tap **Show More** first.
- **Android** — look for **Add to Home Screen** or **Install app** in your browser's menu.

No app store, no account, no download required.
