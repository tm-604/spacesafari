Rating vs. favoriting
The heart (♥) marks who you’re going to see at this festival. Ratings (▲ Love / ● Like / ▼ Not for me) are your honest music opinion, and can be exported together with your notes to other festivals apps. They don’t have to match: you can heart an artist to go see them with friends and still rate them ▼.

Data & Privacy

Your favorites, reminders, discovery checkmarks, ratings, notes, and settings stay only on this device — never uploaded, with no account, sign-in, or tracking. To work, the app makes just two outside requests: it loads its fonts and fetches the weather forecast for the festival site. Neither sends your favorites or any personal data.

Heads up!

This app is designed primarily for phones. On a Mac, Safari's built-in privacy protections may automatically clear your locally-saved favorites and checkmarks after about 7 days without opening this page. To prevent this, save your favorites manually on the Settings page.
